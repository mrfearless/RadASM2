Macros

Macros are used to record keystrokes and mouse movements and play them back. Use Record Macro from the Macro menu to begin recording, all keystrokes and mouse movements will be added until you press Stop Recording in the small dialog that appears. This uses a WH_JOURNALHOOK hook function and is bound by the same limitations as that type of hook. When you are finished recording a Macro save dialog will appear, enter a name as you wish it to appear on the Macro menu, the macro name itself is auto-generated. When you wish to playback the macro, select it from the menu. To arrange your macro menu or delete macros use the Manage option, a dialog similar to the menu edit dialogs will appear.
